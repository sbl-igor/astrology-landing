“Astrology”

What is Astrology?

Astrology is an ancient system that studies the influence of celestial bodies (planets, stars, and their positions) on a person's destiny and character. It claims that the position of celestial bodies at the time of a person's birth can influence their personal qualities and life events.

“Numerology”

What is Numerology?

Numerology is a mystical teaching that studies the influence of numbers on a person's life and destiny. It is believed that numbers associated with the date of birth and name have deep meanings and can reveal important aspects of personality and life path.

“Chirology”

What is Chirology?

Chirology, or palmistry, is the art of palm reading, which suggests that the lines and shapes on the hands can tell about a person's character, destiny, and future. Chirology considers not only the lines on the palm but also the shape of the fingers, mounts, and other signs.
